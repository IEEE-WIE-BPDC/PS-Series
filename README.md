# Placement & Internship Preparation Series (4 Weeks)

A practical 4-week program designed to help university students prepare for internships and placements by building strong profiles, applying strategically, and mastering interviews.

## Structure

**Week 1: LinkedIn & Internship Search**
- Build and optimize LinkedIn
- Learn where and how to find internships
- Networking and recruiter outreach strategies

**Week 2: Resume, Personal Brand & Applications**
- Resume building (even with no experience)
- Personal branding and positioning
- Tailoring applications, cover letters & “Why us?” answers

**Week 3: Interview Mastery**
- Behavioral + technical interview strategies
- Project deep dives and case-style thinking
- Final-round preparation + mock question banks

**Week 4: Resume Review & Feedback**
- Students submit resumes
- Personalized, detailed improvement suggestions

## Outcome

By the end of the series, students will be able to:
- Build a strong professional presence
- Apply strategically to roles
- Confidently handle interviews
- Present their skills, projects, and experiences effectively
